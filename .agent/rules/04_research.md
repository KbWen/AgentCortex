# RULE: RESEARCH & SYNTHESIS (04)

## ⚖️ MODEL SELECTION

- **FLASH**: Use for single-topic searches or finding specific facts (input < 200 tokens).
- **PRO**: Use for comparing complex libraries, deep architectural research, or cross-verifying >3 sources.

## 📋 RESEARCH PROTOCOL

1. **Source Discovery**: Identify primary keywords and related concepts.
2. **Deep Crawl**: Prioritize official docs, GitHub issues, and RFCs.
3. **Comparison Matrix**: Always list alternatives with Pros/Cons if evaluating tech.
4. **Synthesis**: Direct answer first, followed by supporting evidence.

## 🚫 RESTRICTIONS

- Avoid outdated forums (>3 years old) unless legacy support is requested.
- Never cite unverified "AI-generated" snippets without verification.
