# SKILL: RESEARCH TIPS

- **Efficient Sourcing**:
  - Use `site:github.com` or `site:docs.microsoft.com`.
  - Check "Issues" or "Discussions" for real-world edge cases.
- **Verification**:
  - Cross-reference at least two independent sources for high-risk decisions.
- **Avoid**: Outdated docs; check for "Last Updated" or "Version" tags.
